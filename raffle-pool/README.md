## The idea

5% raffle contribution: Takes 5% of swap output amounts
• Commit-reveal gameplay:
• Player 1: Swaps with a commitment hash (move + salt)
• Player 2: Joins by posting their move directly
• Player 1: Reveals move to determine winner
• Prize distribution: Winner takes combined contributions
• Timeouts:
• Player 1 can refund if Player 2 doesn't join within 60 seconds
• Player 2 can claim if Player 1 doesn't reveal within 60 seconds
